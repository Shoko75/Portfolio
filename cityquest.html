About

CityQuest Tour is the app that embarks on an interactive experience to view the major sightseeing of Vancouver in the form of a self-directed treasure hunt tour. As you follow the suggested route on our map, you will look for numbers to unlock an audio track who unveiled the history of Yaletown and Gastown.

Challenges

It was the first time to build the iOS app. Therefore, Everything was challenging for me such as MVC structure, Core Data and Audio implementing. In addition, needed to build as possible as expandable easily for able to add some additional tours in the future.

Menu

Choose a tour that feels interesting on this page. Currently, there is just one tour however we're going to add some more tours. This page is built by Collection View for easy to implement more tours in the future.

Tour Detail

You can know about the tour's detail on this page. I created that the user understands easily about the tour by classifying information such as the duration or amount of participants. in addition can check all places by displaying pictures.

Audio

In the audio track, there are some quizzes related to each place. By press the lock key button and type the answer, the place is going to unlock. As a result, you can enjoy by unveiling the history of the place.

Map

Show the all tour route and current location. Therefore you can easily follow the tour even don't know about Vancouver. In addition, it's possible to recognize the place name by pressing the number on the map.

Progress of the tours

Possible to check the progress of each tour. By pressing the Start button, you can go back to the tour easily.
